# Oasis Services

## Positioning

Oasis Engineering is the independent layer between manufacturer information and a real, approvable foundation path.

This repo is designed to make that role clear.

For the visual version, see [Service Flow](service-flow.md).

## What Oasis Engineering Can Offer

- early feasibility and reality checks
- site-specific foundation family recommendation
- review of current BOXABL source documents
- permit-path guidance
- coordination with geotechnical input
- PE-reviewed structural design support
- builder-facing detail clarification
- utility, elevation, and sequence coordination

## Typical Engagement Ladder

### 1. Fit Check

Best for owners who want to know if the site, jurisdiction, and unit concept are aligned before they waste time.

### 2. Foundation Strategy

Best for owners or builders choosing between slab, stem wall, pier-and-beam, or grade beam approaches.

### 3. Permit Package Support

Best for projects that need site-specific sheets, PE review, and clean handoff into permit or construction.

### 4. Construction Support

Best for jobs where field tolerances, utility conflicts, or contractor questions are likely.

## Why This Position Works

Manufacturer documents tend to answer product questions.

Owners still need someone to answer project questions.

That is where Oasis becomes the specialist.
